Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign up?

Sign up to access full library features such as Authentication codes download or ability to like and comment.

How do I change my password?

Under the Password section, click or tap Change. Enter your current password in the first text box and then click Next. For Windows 10 users, enter your new password twice to verify that you've typed it correctly.

How can i download authentication code?

Register to access the authentication codes.

Can i connect with my github account?

Yes, you can absolutely connect with your Github account.

Is there a team i can work with?

You can send a mail to us, we'd get back to you if a team is available.

Is the code free to download?

Download is only available to registered users.

How can i reset my password?

On the Login page, there's a password reset link. Follow the steps to successfully reset your password.

Need more help?

Want to talk to us or you have anything to share with us, click on the contact us button and fill the form on the page, our team member will respond to you as quickly as possible
